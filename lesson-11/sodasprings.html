Nice Weather and Rich History

No we don't have soda in our springs but we do have nice weather and a rich history. Here in Soda Springs you can visit our geyser, enjoy Octoberfest or maybe drink soda at the number of springs that we have. Maybe you are more interested in our history. Well you can see lots of old buildings and even a cabin built for Brigham Young. If you are looking to sit back, relax, maybe row a boat, then come enjoy our fishing spots just outside of town.
