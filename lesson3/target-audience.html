Target Audience

The target audience will mainly appeal to adults from the age 21 and beyond and for both genders. That live in the state of Arizona or are planning to visit Arizona.

Two Personas

Jackie Blue: She is someone who lives in Tombstone, Arizona but has family in Mesa, Arizona and Storm Center, Arizona. She likes to check that the weather is nice for her and her family and likes to make sure there is nothing bad going on around her area or her families.

David Springer: He is from Missouri looking to fly to Arizona. He likes to check the weather and plan what it will be like a week out. In addition, he likes to check to see what is nearby or what to do during the week he'll be out.

Scenarios

Jackie notices clouds are forming in the sky in the middle of dead winter. She hops on the site to check what the weather will be like for her town in Tombstone. She hops on the site and the first thing she sees is a map with the highs and lows for all of Arizona. She is able to pinpoint her location and figure out that she may be receiving rain in her area. She decides to click on her location and it reveals some news or events near or surronding her.
