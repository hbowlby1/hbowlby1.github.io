A&P Nursery

Website

AP Nursery has been around since the 70's in Arizona and have 3 locations in Mesa, Gilbert and Queen Creek. They specialize in tools, seeds, cactus and fruit trees. They even offer a tropical fruit section that is open during the best times to plant. If you want advice on planting and maintaing plants the employees there will keep you informed. Don't rely on the internet to tell your clients on what to plant or maintain. Go to them and they will tell you all that you need to know.
